Sales questions

How do I get started with YMLK Technologies?

You can request a free consultation with one of our experts to discuss your requirements and receive a tailored solution that meets your needs. Our experts will assess your requirements and provide you with an estimate of the cost for completing the project.
Once you receive the quote, you can decide if you would like to move forward with us or not. We strive to provide the best services at the most competitive prices. To get a free quote today, please get in touch with us.

Do you provide consulting services?

Yes, we have a team of experienced professionals who provide consulting services to ensure that our products are meeting your business goals. We can also provide advice and assistance on how to use our services to improve your workflow and efficiency.

What is your customer support policy?

We are available to provide help and advice whenever needed. You can reach us via telephone, email, or our social media channels. Additionally, we have online resources available on our website that provide useful information and answers to commonly asked questions.

Do you provide maintenance and support services?

Yes, we provide ongoing maintenance and support services for all our products, including mobile apps, web apps and websites. Our team is available to provide help and advice whenever needed.

Which platforms do you support?

We provide support for all major web browsers, mobile devices (Android and iOS) and operating systems.

Do you accept custom projects?

Yes, we are open to working on custom projects. We have an experienced and highly skilled team of developers that can bring your ideas to life.

How long does a Mobile app, Web app or Website development usually take?

The length of time needed for the development of any project depends on various factors, such as the complexity of the project, the technologies being used, if all resources have been made readily available and SCOPE CREEP. On average, it typically takes around 4-8 weeks to complete a project. NB: This is just an average time, all projects we have done so far have been completed in a far less duration than the time mentioned above.

What is workflow automation?

Workflow automation is the process of automating tedious tasks and processes in order to reduce manual inputs, improve accuracy, and increase efficiency and productivity.

How secure are your services?

We take security very seriously and our services are designed to be secure and compliant with industry standards. Our software products are built with robust security measures and we use secure protocols and encryption technologies for all data transfers.

Do you provide training for your products?

Yes, we offer comprehensive one-on-one training for all our products. We also provide ongoing maintenance and support services to ensure that your products remain up-to-date and functional.

What is your turnaround time?

We usually complete projects within the agreed time frame. The total time for a project depends on the size and complexity of the project.

Do you offer hosting services?

Yes, we provide secure and reliable hosting services for all our websites.

What are the payment terms?

We require a 50% deposit prior to beginning the project. The remaining balance is due when the project is completed.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all forms of Electronic payments but most preferably, bank transfers.

Is there a money-back guarantee?

Yes, we offer a money-back guarantee for any of our services. If work has already been started on your project, you will be refunded 50% of your initial deposit amount. However, if no service has been rendered yet, you will be refunded the full amount minus any transaction fees.
Please note: if any of the money has already been used to purchase resources for your project, you will not be refunded for those costs, You will only be refunded the remainder.
For example, if you have paid us a 50% deposit of GHS 500 for a project and we have already purchased resources for the project using GHS 200 of the deposit, we will only refund GHS 300, which is the remainder of the original deposit amount (GHS 500). However, if no work has been done on the project yet, you will be refunded the full amount of GHS500 minus any transaction fees.

Do you offer any discounts ?

Yes, we do offer discounts for large projects or long-term contracts. Please contact us for more information.

Are there any extra fees or costs associated with your services?

We strive to keep our fees and costs competitive, so there are no added costs or fees associated with our services, EXCEPT THERE IS A SCOPE CREEP.
Scope creep refers to/occurs when a client adds new provisions, tasks, or deliverables to a project, that are outside the existing (agreed) scope of the project at any point after the project begins.
